Privacy Policy for Prize Catalog

Last Updated: October 8, 2025

1. Information Collected

This Prize Catalog application, powered by Google Apps Script, collects and uses minimal personal information necessary for its function.

  • **Authentication Data:** Your Google email address is collected upon sign-in to verify your identity and authorize access to the catalog.
  • **User Data:** Your name and current point total are retrieved from an internal spreadsheet system (Google Sheet) associated with this application.
  • **Transaction Data:** Records of your prize redemption history, including the items ordered, total points spent, and transaction timestamp, are stored for internal tracking and fulfillment purposes.

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used exclusively for the operation of the Prize Catalog, including:

  • Verifying user eligibility and points balance.
  • Processing and fulfilling prize redemption requests.
  • Maintaining accurate records of point balances and transaction history.

3. Data Security and Sharing

All data is stored within your organization's Google environment (Google Sheets). We do not share any personal or transaction data with external third parties. Access to the underlying data is limited to authorized personnel responsible for system maintenance and prize fulfillment.
